Presidential Graduate School (PGS)

About Presidential Graduate School

Presidential Graduate School (PGS) is a prestigious educational institution located in Thapagaun, New Baneshwor, Kathmandu, Nepal. It was established in 2011 with a mission to deliver quality higher education in Business, Technology, and related fields.

The school aims to promote practical learning, innovation, and research while preparing students for leadership roles in their chosen careers.

Academic Programs

PGS offers a range of undergraduate and graduate programs including:

  • Bachelor of Science in Information Technology (BSIT)
  • Bachelor of Science in Artificial Intelligence (BS AI)
  • Bachelor of Science in Cybersecurity (BS Cybersec)
  • Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA)
  • Master of Science in Information Technology (MSIT)
  • Master of Business Administration (MBA) in various fields
